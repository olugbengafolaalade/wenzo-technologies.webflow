Our Engagement Models

Save time and get more value for your money

By choosing a plan you get a dedicated tech team committed to make your dreams a reality and deliver your web and mobile products.

Equity Model – Build Now, Grow Together
For founders with visionary ideas but limited resources, our Equity Model provides the technical foundation you need to launch. We invest in your startup by designing and developing your technology infrastructure in exchange for equity. This model allows you to focus on business growth while we handle the tech.

Best for: Early-stage startups with strong market potential but limited capital.
Cashflow Model – Flexible Payment Plans
If you're ready to invest in your product, our Cashflow Model offers a structured, milestone-based payment plan. You maintain full ownership of your business while gaining access to our expert development team, ensuring a high-quality product that scales with your vision.

Best for: Founders with capital to invest who want full ownership of their technology.
Hybrid Model – A Balanced Approach
For startups that need both financial flexibility and strategic support, our Hybrid Model blends equity and cash. This approach allows us to share in the risk and reward while ensuring you retain more ownership and control over your product.

Best for: Startups looking for a customized approach to funding and development.
